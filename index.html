Doctor of Chiropractic

In my first career I was a chiropractor. I worked in two different clinics, one focused on sports injuries, and the other on injuries following motor vehicle accidents. I greatly enjoyed treating patients, helping them understand their bodies, and helping them discover what their true goals were and how to achieve them. I learned how to work as a team with multiple medical specialties to get the patient healthy as fast as possible. Communication with the patient is key, and it was something I learned continuously as I practiced.

Ultimate (frisbee)

I have played the sport of Ultimate since I was 18. I started playing when I went to college and became team captain my junior year. I even moved across the country from Iowa to Oregon to play at Oregon State. I have played on over a dozen different teams in Iowa, Minnesota, Oregon, and Colorado. I have also started coaching over the last few years for both high school and college, most recently as a coach at Colorado College, where we made nationals in 2021 for the first time in five years! Coaching has taught me a lot about communicating with people, meeting people where they are with skills and expectations, and how to get the most out of people.
